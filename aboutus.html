About Sarap ng Lutong Bahay

Founded in 1995, Sarap ng Lutong Bahay is a beloved family-owned restaurant dedicated to bringing the authentic taste of home-cooked Filipino meals to your table. Our passion is to serve dishes made with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and a whole lot of love.

We believe food is more than just sustenance—it’s a way to connect, share stories, and celebrate the warmth of Filipino hospitality. Whether it’s our signature Pork Adobo or hearty Sinigang, each dish is prepared with care to remind you of the comfort of home.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, affordable, and wholesome Filipino meals that bring families and friends together, keeping the rich culinary traditions alive for generations to come.

Our Values

  • Quality: Using only the freshest ingredients for every dish.
  • Authenticity: Preserving traditional Filipino flavors and cooking methods.
  • Community: Building relationships with our customers and supporting local suppliers.
  • Warmth: Creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Meet Our Founder: Maria Dela Cruz

Maria started Sarap ng Lutong Bahay in her humble kitchen with a dream to share her family's recipes with the world. Over the years, her dedication and love for cooking have turned the restaurant into a cherished destination for food lovers.

When she’s not in the kitchen, Maria enjoys gardening, storytelling, and passing down culinary secrets to the next generation.
